A Timeless Moscow
Institution

Nestled in the heart of Moscow's historic Tverskoy district, Café Pushkin has
been a beacon of Russian hospitality and culinary excellence for over two
decades. Our 18th-century inspired interior, combined with contemporary
culinary techniques, creates an unforgettable dining experience.

From our signature Russian breakfast to our internationally acclaimed
dinner menu, every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the
timeless spirit of Moscow.
